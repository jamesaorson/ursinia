Bible Reading Plan - Primer

The main idea is to read through the following books, in order, and a certain number of pages/chapters a day.

For the first month, read the corresponding Proverb to the calendar day of the month. So January 1st, you read Proverbs 1, and January 31st you read Proverbs 31. After the first month, this slot should be filled up with memorization practice. Pick a passage or passages and store it in your heart.

Daily reading counts are still in development.

  2. Exodus - Know that the LORD saves his people
