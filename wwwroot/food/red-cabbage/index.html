Red Cabbage

Images

Ingredients

  • 1 head (~1kg) red cabbage
  • 2 tablespoons Margarine, butter, or oil
  • 1/4 to 1/2 deciliters of syrup
  • 3 tablespoons vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 dash of pepper
  • 1/2 deciliter of currant juice or red wine
  • 2 apples (leave out for the brown cabbage recipe, but the card mentions it for the original red cabbage)

Directions

  1. NOTE: For brown cabbage, replace the red cabbage with white cabbage, reduce the vinegar slightly and exclude the apples.
  2. Trim and shred the cabbage nicely.
  3. Melt the shortening in a saucepan.
  4. Add to the bowl and drizzle with the syrup.
  5. Let it all sizzle while stirring for a while.
  6. Peel and cut the apples into wedges and add them at the same time with the vinegar.
  7. Cook the kettle until soft, about 1 1/2 hours.
  8. Stir the pot, there and there (occasionally).
  9. Season with salt, pepper and possibly juice or red wine.
