---
title: Chi'k'n Alfredo
---


## Ingredients

- 15.5 oz alfredo sauce
- 8 oz pasta, cooked and drained
- 8 oz Mindful Chik'n
- Boiling water

## Directions

1.  Cook your pasta
2.  Cook your chik'n in a cast iron pan
3.  When the chik'n is browned, add the alfredo to the cast iron and
    heat till bubbling
4.  Spice and season!
5.  Turn off the heat
6.  Add the cooked and drained pasta
7.  Mix well
