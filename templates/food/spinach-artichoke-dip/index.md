## Ingredients

- Prepared cashew cheese
- 14 oz. spinach, frozen or fresh
- 1 can (14oz.) artichoke hearts in brine, drained and finely chopped

## Directions

1.  Preheat oven to 400 degrees fahrenheit
2.  Dice the artichoke hearts
3.  Wash and chop the spinach leaves
4.  Combine in a food processor with the cashew cheese
5.  In a medium baking dish, add the mixture
6.  Cover
7.  Bake 10 minutes
8.  Uncover
9.  Bake 10 minutes
10. Continue adding time as needed

## Notes

- Reheat at 375 degrees for 10 to 15 minutes
- Store for up to 4 days in a refrigerated, covered container
