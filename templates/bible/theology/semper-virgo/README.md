# Semper Virgo

Research on the perpetual virginity of Mary (*semper virgo* — "ever virgin").

## Status

Scaffolded. Research not yet begun.

## Scope

- Biblical evidence for and against
- Patristic witness (Fathers who affirmed, Fathers who denied or were silent)
- Reformation-era positions (Luther, Calvin, Zwingli, later Reformed)
- Confessional Baptist evaluation from the 1689 framework
