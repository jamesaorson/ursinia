Ursinia Charter

Maintain Order

In life, there is a choice between order and chaos at most moments. God is the only true being of order, and His will is the only certain course for orderly action. Therefore, consult the Lord in all you do, adhering to the groans of the spirit. When order seems counter cultural, we are to remember Romans 1:16.

Through marriage

Papa's marriage to Mama demonstrates the profound mystery of Christ's love of the church (Ephesians 5:31-32). All that we do can be seen as a reflection of Christ and His love for the church. It is by this marriage that the Orson family was founded and continues to orient itself.

In Christ

As we live, let our focus and effort be for Christ and His kingdom (Philippians 1:21-24), and treat our lives as forfeit to Christ who redeemed us (Matthew 16:25).
